⬡ See KEEL in Your Wallet

Your balance always shows here in the app — adding KEEL to your wallet app is optional. One-tap works on desktop MetaMask; MetaMask mobile usually blocks auto-adding testnet tokens, so on a phone use the quick manual steps below.

📲 Add manually (works on any wallet)
  1. In your wallet, switch the network to Polygon Amoy.
  2. Open Import tokens → Custom token.
  3. Paste the token contract address below (symbol & decimals auto-fill).
  4. Confirm — KEEL now shows in your wallet.
